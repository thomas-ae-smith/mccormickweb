The existing building is asymmetrical but the setting with drive at the back and view over lawns to the front suggests a balance which has been emphasised by the design of these two new extensions. By siting the two new rooms away from the existing buildings two new courtyards are formed. The building form has a traditional symmetry imposed, giving it the balance it lacked, although the new wings have a subtly modern feel.
